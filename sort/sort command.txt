sort -k2: sort dựa vào cột 2
sort -k1: sort dựa vào cột 1


minhql@ubuntu:~$ sort -k2 abc
monkey  ax
banana  ba
apple   con
china   meo
apple   x
minhql@ubuntu:~$ sort -k1 abc
apple   con
apple   x
banana  ba
china   meo
monkey  ax
minhql@ubuntu:~$ cat abc
apple   con
china   meo
banana  ba
apple   x
monkey  ax
